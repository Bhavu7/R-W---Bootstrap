About Us

Shri D N Institute of Computer Applications

Shri Dadabhai Naoroji Institute of Computer Application, Anand, formally known as Shri D N Institute of Computer Application (DNICA), was established in April 2011. It is affiliated with Sardar Patel University, Vallabh Vidyanagar, and managed by Charotar Education Society, Anand.

The society was founded on 16th April 1916 by the visionary Shri Motibhai Amin Saheb to advance higher education in the Charotar Pradesh, the region between the Mahi and Vatrak rivers, extending from Vasad to Kheda.

Message From Principal's Desk

Dr. Maulik Pandya - Principal

What is education? Children's minds and bodies are being developed healthily. I think that computers are smart tools for rapid development, and I firmly believe this. The benefits will be discussed.

Thanks to computers and the internet, you have access to all kinds of information. In other words, the entire world is just a click away, and it's easy to access any information, idea, or thought. You are aware that education is now more advanced than it was with just books thanks to computers.

Remember the bygone era when you had to flip through entire books to find the smallest of details? Currently, you can increase your knowledge by giving computers a few keywords. The computer is a repository for all available data on everything in one place.

Our institute is committed to providing students with the latest technological tools and knowledge to prepare them for the digital future. We focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to ensure our graduates are industry-ready.
