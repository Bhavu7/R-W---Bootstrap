About Scholarships

At DNICA, we are committed to making quality education accessible through various scholarship programs. These scholarships are designed to support meritorious students, those with financial needs, and individuals from underrepresented communities.

Explore our scholarship offerings below and learn how to apply. For further assistance, contact our scholarship office via the contact section.

Available Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarship

Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance in their 10+2 examinations or previous semesters.

  • Eligibility: Minimum 85% in 10+2 or CGPA 8.5
  • Coverage: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver
  • Application Deadline: July 15, 2025
