About Our Placement Cell

The Placement Cell at DNICA is dedicated to bridging the gap between students and industry. We provide comprehensive support through career counseling, resume building, mock interviews, and skill development workshops to prepare students for successful careers in IT and related fields.

Our strong network with leading companies ensures that our students secure placements in reputed organizations. The Placement Cell also organizes campus drives, job fairs, and industry interactions to enhance employability and provide real-world exposure.
