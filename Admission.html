Admission Process

At DNICA, we offer a streamlined admission process to help you join our esteemed BCA and BSc CA & IT programs. Admissions are open for the 2025 academic year, and we welcome students who are passionate about pursuing a career in technology.

Our process includes eligibility checks, submission of required documents, and an entrance test (if applicable). We provide guidance at every step to ensure a smooth application experience.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

A 3-year undergraduate program focusing on computer science fundamentals, programming, and software development.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks
  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Intake: 120 seats
